HubSpot Marketing Automation:
The Ultimate Setup Guide for 2025

REVO-SYS · APRIL 2025 · 10 MIN READ
Quick Answer

HubSpot marketing automation uses Workflows to trigger emails, update properties, notify reps, and move contacts through lifecycle stages — all based on behaviour and attributes. The most impactful automations are lead nurturing sequences, MQL-to-sales handoffs, and re-engagement campaigns.

The 7 HubSpot Workflows Every B2B Company Needs

1. Lead Nurturing Sequence — Top of Funnel
TRIGGER:Form submission (any lead magnet)
  1. Immediately: Send thank-you email with content download
  2. Day 2: Educational email — "The problem most [ICP] teams face"
  3. Day 5: Case study email — how a client achieved [result]
  4. Day 9: Social proof email — testimonials + ROI stats
  5. Day 14: Soft CTA — "Ready to see how this works for you?"
  6. If demo booked: exit workflow, enrol in sales handoff workflow
2. MQL → Sales Handoff
TRIGGER:Lead score ≥ MQL threshold
  1. Update lifecycle stage to "Marketing Qualified Lead"
  2. Assign contact to sales rep (round-robin or territory-based)
  3. Send internal email to rep with contact summary
  4. Create task: "Follow up within 24 hours"
  5. If rep doesn't log activity in 48 hours: send reminder + notify manager
3. Demo / Meeting Booked Follow-up
TRIGGER:Meeting booked via Meetings link
  1. Immediately: Send confirmation with meeting details + prep agenda
  2. Day before: Reminder with "3 things to bring to our conversation"
  3. After meeting: Follow-up with recap + next steps template
  4. Update contact lifecycle to "Sales Qualified Lead"
4. Re-engagement Campaign
TRIGGER:No email opens in 90 days + Lead/Subscriber lifecycle
  1. Day 1: Re-engagement email — "Still interested in [value prop]?"
  2. Day 7 (if no open): Different subject line — "Quick question, [First Name]"
  3. Day 14 (if still no open): "Removing you from our list" breakup email
  4. If no engagement: mark as "Unqualified", suppress from future sends
  5. If engages: re-enrol in nurture sequence
5. Onboarding Automation (Post-Sale)
TRIGGER:Deal stage = Closed Won
  1. Immediately: Welcome email with onboarding checklist
  2. Day 3: "Getting started" video walkthrough
  3. Day 7: Check-in email from CS rep
  4. Day 14: "Have you tried [key feature]?" activation email
  5. Day 30: NPS survey + health check
6. Pricing Page Visitor Alert
TRIGGER:Contact visits /pricing page
  1. Add contact to "High Intent" list
  2. Increase lead score by +20
  3. If assigned to sales rep: create task "Hot prospect visited pricing — reach out today"
  4. If not yet assigned: enrol in direct CTA email sequence
7. Data Enrichment & Hygiene
TRIGGER:Contact created (any source)
  1. Attempt enrichment via HubSpot Insights or connected enrichment tool
  2. If company name missing: flag for manual review
  3. Set lifecycle stage based on source
  4. Assign to geographic territory if configured

Pro tip: Always set a goal on your workflows. When a contact achieves the goal (books a meeting, reaches a lifecycle stage), they exit automatically. This prevents contacts from receiving irrelevant emails after they've already converted.

Frequently Asked Questions

What can you automate with HubSpot?
HubSpot can automate: lead nurturing emails, lifecycle stage updates, lead scoring, contact property management, internal sales notifications, deal creation, meeting follow-ups, and customer onboarding workflows.
How do I set up a lead nurturing workflow in HubSpot?
Go to Automation > Workflows, create a Contact-based workflow, set your enrollment trigger, then add email actions with time delays. Use if/then branches to personalise based on engagement behaviour.
What HubSpot plan do you need for marketing automation?
Basic workflows are available on Marketing Hub Starter. Full workflow automation with branching and goal-setting requires Marketing Hub Professional (~$800/month) or Enterprise (~$3,600/month).
How many workflows should a B2B company have?
Start with 5–7 core workflows (lead nurture, MQL handoff, demo follow-up, re-engagement, onboarding, data hygiene). Most mature HubSpot portals have 20–50 active workflows.
