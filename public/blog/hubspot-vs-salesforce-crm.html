HubSpot vs Salesforce:
Which CRM Wins in 2025?

REVO-SYS · APRIL 2025 · 9 MIN READ
Quick Answer

Choose HubSpot if you're an SMB or mid-market company that wants fast setup, built-in marketing automation, and lower total cost. Choose Salesforce if you're an enterprise with highly complex custom processes, a large Salesforce admin team, and deep legacy system integration requirements.

The Core Trade-off

HubSpot and Salesforce are both world-class CRMs, but they're optimised for very different buyers. HubSpot is built for speed-to-value and usability. Salesforce is built for maximum flexibility and enterprise scale. The right choice depends almost entirely on your company's size, technical resources, and operational complexity.

Pricing: The Real Story

HubSpot Pricing

  • Free CRM: Unlimited users, core contact and deal management
  • Sales Hub Starter: ~$20/user/month — email sequences, basic automation
  • Sales Hub Professional: ~$90/user/month — forecasting, playbooks, custom reporting
  • Sales Hub Enterprise: ~$150/user/month — predictive lead scoring, advanced permissions

Salesforce Pricing

  • Professional: $80/user/month — most commonly used tier
  • Enterprise: $165/user/month — workflow automation, advanced customisation
  • Unlimited: $330/user/month — full suite with Einstein AI
  • Hidden costs: Implementation ($20k–$200k), ongoing admin ($80–120k/yr), add-ons for marketing, CPQ, service cloud

Real-world cost: A 20-person sales team on HubSpot Pro runs ~$1,800/month all-in. The same team on Salesforce Enterprise with a part-time admin typically runs $4,500–7,000/month. Over 3 years, HubSpot typically saves $150k–300k at this company size.

When to Choose HubSpot

  • You're an SMB or mid-market company (under 500 employees)
  • Your marketing team wants to own the CRM without heavy IT involvement
  • You need marketing automation and CRM in one platform
  • You don't have a dedicated Salesforce admin on staff
  • You want to be live within 30 days, not 6 months

When to Choose Salesforce

  • You're an enterprise (1,000+ employees) with complex org-wide processes
  • You have dedicated Salesforce admins and developers on staff
  • You need deep integration with legacy ERP systems (SAP, Oracle)
  • Your sales process has highly custom objects, workflows, or territory hierarchies
  • Your investors or board expect Salesforce (common in later-stage B2B SaaS)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is HubSpot better than Salesforce?
HubSpot is better for SMBs and mid-market companies that need fast time-to-value, built-in marketing tools, and lower total cost. Salesforce is better for enterprises with complex custom processes and dedicated Salesforce admin resources.
How much does HubSpot cost vs Salesforce?
HubSpot Sales Hub Professional runs ~$90/user/month. Salesforce Enterprise runs $165/user/month plus significant hidden costs for implementation, add-ons, and admin. Real all-in costs typically make Salesforce 2–3× more expensive for mid-market teams.
Can you migrate from Salesforce to HubSpot?
Yes. HubSpot has a dedicated migration path and certified partners. Key steps: map data objects, clean records before export, and run both systems in parallel during the 60–90 day transition.
Which CRM is easier to use?
HubSpot consistently wins on ease of use. Its UI is more intuitive, adoption rates are higher, and new reps can onboard themselves without extended training. Salesforce requires more structured training and admin support.
